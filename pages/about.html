Hi, I'm Lei Gabriel 👋

Born on May 05, 2005

I'm a visual creative and frontend development enthusiast with roots in graphic design. I’m deeply passionate about storytelling, aesthetics, and building things that connect with people emotionally and meaningfully.

I come from a background where stability isn’t always guaranteed, so I’ve learned to value resourcefulness and patience. Design and code aren’t just creative outlets — they’re survival tools.

Life hasn’t always been easy, but every challenge has taught me resilience. I’m working to merge traditional art with modern tech to build meaningful digital experiences.

My work is guided by empathy, curiosity, and intention. Whether I’m building a responsive UI, crafting a visual identity, or sketching ideas on paper, I approach each project with care and commitment. I believe design should serve people, tell stories, and make complexity feel simple.

I'm currently focused on expanding my skills in frontend development — particularly HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and responsive design — while still grounding my creativity in strong visual principles. My long-term goal is to merge traditional art with modern tech to create digital experiences that feel human.

Outside of design and development, I’m someone who values quiet moments, meaningful conversations, and staying grounded in faith and community. Every day is a learning experience, and I’m here to grow — both as a creator and as a person.
